About Mary

Hello! I have been taking photos professionally since 2008 and I think I am even more obsessed with it now than I was back then. There is nothing more fun for me than photographing happy people and creating memories for families to have for years and years. I strive to create portraits that document genuine emotion and connections between people. I especially love to make people laugh. A lot of photographers choose to specialize in one area, but I haven't found it in my heart to do that yet. I love switching things up and trying new things. That's why you'll find such a variety of photos here on my site.

Besides photography, I am obsessed with my husband, our three children, and our miniature schnauzer. We have a tortoise and a bearded dragon that are pretty fun as well. I do photography part-time, as I am a full-time school psychologist. I am passionate about helping children with disabilities succeed. I hope you'll explore my site and get into contact with me so we can create some beautiful photos!
